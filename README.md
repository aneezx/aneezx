**Security & Infrastructure**
- **Overview:** Integrate cybersecurity controls into IT infrastructure and service management to reduce risk, improve resilience, and enable responsive operations across cloud and on-premises environments.
- **Core Focus Areas:**
	- **Identity & Access Management (IAM):** Enforce least privilege, MFA, role-based access, and centralized identity (Azure AD) with entitlements reviews.
	- **Network & Perimeter Security:** Segmentation, zero-trust microperimeters, firewalls, NSGs, secure VPNs, and secure service endpoints.
	- **Endpoint & Workload Protection:** EDR/XDR on endpoints and runtime protection for containers and VMs; automated threat containment.
	- **Configuration & Patch Management:** Automated patch pipelines, desired-state configuration (IaC), and drift detection to reduce exposure windows.
	- **Logging, Monitoring & Detection (SIEM):** Centralized logs, telemetry enrichment, alert tuning, and SIEM (Azure Sentinel, Splunk) for detection and hunting.
	- **Vulnerability & Asset Management:** Continuous scanning, prioritized remediation, and accurate CMDB mapping to connect vulnerabilities to services and owners.
	- **Change & Incident Management (ITSM):** Integrate security checks into change workflows, maintain runbooks in ServiceNow, and ensure fast incident triage and communication.
	- **Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery:** Secure backups, tested RTO/RPO plans, and recovery playbooks that consider cyberattack scenarios.
	- **Compliance & Auditability:** Controls mapping to frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001, CIS), automated evidence collection, and regular audit readiness checks.
	- **Automation & Orchestration:** Use IaC (Terraform/ARM/Bicep), automation (Ansible, GitHub Actions), and SOAR for repeatable secure operations.

- **Practical Best Practices:**
	- Use infrastructure-as-code and peer review for all changes; require security gate checks in CI/CD pipelines.
	- Instrument services with structured logs and correlate telemetry across network, host, and cloud services.
	- Maintain an accurate CMDB and link it to incident and vulnerability workflows for faster ownership and remediation.
	- Apply least privilege across service and machine identities; rotate credentials and prefer managed identities.
	- Run regular tabletop exercises and post-incident reviews to improve runbooks and SLAs.

- **Suggested Tooling & Integrations:**
	- Cloud-native: Azure Sentinel, Defender for Cloud, Azure AD Identity Protection.
	- SIEM & Observability: Splunk, Datadog, Elastic Stack.
	- ITSM: ServiceNow, Jira Service Management.
	- Automation & IaC: Terraform, ARM/Bicep, Ansible, GitHub Actions.
	- Endpoint: Microsoft Defender for Endpoint, CrowdStrike, SentinelOne.

- **Quick Security Checklist (For Service Owners):**
	- Ensure service owners and on-call rotations are defined in the CMDB.
	- Enable centralized logging and retention for critical services.
	- Verify MFA and conditional access for admin roles.
	- Confirm automated patching and vulnerability scanning schedules.
	- Validate backup encryption, access controls, and recovery test results.
